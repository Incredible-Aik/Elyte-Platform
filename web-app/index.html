About Elyte

Connecting Ghana through technology and transportation

Elyte is Ghana's homegrown ride-sharing platform, designed specifically for the unique needs of our communities. We understand the challenges of transportation in Ghana and have built solutions that work for everyone - from smartphone users in Accra to feature phone users in rural areas.

Our platform integrates seamlessly with local payment systems and provides multiple access methods to ensure that quality transportation is accessible to all Ghanaians.
